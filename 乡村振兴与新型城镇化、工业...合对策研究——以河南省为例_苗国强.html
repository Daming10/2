乡村振兴与新型城镇化、工业化融合对策研究  
——以河南省为例  
苗国强  
（洛阳理工学院 马克思主义学院，河南 洛阳 471023）  
摘
要：乡村振兴是新时代实现“三农”现代化的总抓手，河南省城镇化率已超过50%，但农村人口数量  
仍有5000万左右，其生产生活场所依然在广大农村。虽然工业增加值逐年上升，但信息化程度总体偏低，新  
兴产业增加值仍有较大发掘空间。“三农”问题经由“新农村”—“城镇化”—“新型城镇化”，改革发展成效明  
显，但仍然与实现农业农村现代化宏伟目标有差距。基于河南省的客观省情，只有将乡村振兴、新型城镇  
化、新型工业化进行深度融合发展，以“三农”为基础稳住底盘，以工业为动能拓展和延伸产业链，走三者融  
合发展之路，实现乡村全面振兴，才能推进河南经济社会发展和全面建成小康社会。  
关键词：新型城镇化；新型工业化；乡村振兴；融合发展

Countermeasure study on the Integration of Rural Revitalization, New Urbanization and  
Industrialization  
—A Case Study of Henan Province  
Miao Guoqiang  
Abstract: Rural revitalization is the vital means of realizing the modernization of agriculture, rural areas  
and farmers in the new era. The urbanization rate of Henan Province has exceeded 50%, but the number of  
rural population is still about 50 million, and the production and living place is still in the vast rural areas.  
Industrial added value is increasing year by year, but the informationization degree is generally low, and there is  
still much room to explore the added value of emerging industries. Reform and development efficiency relating  
to agriculture, rural areas and farmers has achieved orderly growth through new rural areas to urbanization, then  
to new urbanization. However, there is still a certain gap to realize the grand goal of modernization of agriculture  
and rural areas. Based on the objective provincial conditions of Henan Province, we should deeply integrate  
rural revitalization, new urbanization and new industrialization, stabilize the chassis on agriculture, rural areas  
and farmers, expand and extend industrial chains with industry as the motive force. Only by deep integration,  
can we realize the comprehensive revitalization of the rural, areas and promote the overall economic and social  
development of Henan Province and build a well-off society in an in an all-round way.  
Key words: New Urbanization; New Industrialization; Rural Revitalization; Integrated Development
